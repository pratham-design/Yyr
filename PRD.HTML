Product Requirements Document

Overview

The Product Requirements Document (PRD) outlines the specifications and features required for the development of a new car model. It serves as a blueprint for engineers, designers, and other stakeholders involved in the manufacturing process.

Key Features

  • Advanced safety features
  • Efficient fuel consumption
  • Integration of smart technology
  • Luxurious interior design
  • Environmental sustainability

Timeline

  • Concept Design: 3 months
  • Engineering and Prototyping: 9 months
  • Testing and Refinement: 4 months
  • Production Preparation: 2 months

The development of the new car model is expected to take approximately 18 months from concept to production.

Budget

The estimated budget for the development of the new car model is $50 million. Funds will be allocated for research and development, prototype production, testing and validation, and manufacturing setup.
